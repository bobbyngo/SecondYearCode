~2.0 Layouts~

Layouts are the most important part of a Swing application. They determine how your application will look, and function. Complete this section to find out about how to manage the placement of components in your application.

~Task 2.2~

How can we make components take up more space? Components have a setPreferredSize() method. This will set the desired size for the component, and frame.pack() will size it to that size or greater.

setPrefferedSize() takes a Dimension object. A Dimension object takes a width and a height in its constructor. Refer to the example in Main.java.

Make the appropriate additions to Main.java to add the JLabels to the frame such that they are all visible and adequately spaced out.
