~2.0 Layouts~

Layouts are the most important part of a Swing application. They determine how your application will look, and function. Complete this section to find out about how to manage the placement of components in your application.

~Task 2.6~

Finally, we can put layouts inside layouts! The key is to wrap your components in JPanels, and change each JPanel's layout manager as needed.

As a final exercise for this section, give headerPanel a FlowLayout, bodyPanel a GridLayout, and footerPanel a GridBagLayout. The mainPanel should have a BorderLayout.
